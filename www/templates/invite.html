Register as a user.

Before you register your business, you need to first register as a user of TriniBiz. While registering, you will be prompted to choose whether you are a business user (owner, agent) or a regular user.

Business users vs Regular users

Business users are users who own or represent a business registered in TriniBiz. Regular users are simply all other users who do not own or represent a business registered in TriniBiz.

To register your business, please send the following info to invite@trinibiz.co

  • -Name and address of business

  • -Business Email, phone contact and fax # (fax # not required)

  • -Website and Facebook Page (not required)

  • -Services provided

  • -State whether you are an agent, or the owner of the business
