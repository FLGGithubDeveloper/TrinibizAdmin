TriniBiz Admin Center

Different type of users

There are two types of users: Biz user (business owner or service provider) or a regular user. Business owners are users who own or represent a business registered in TriniBiz. Service Providers are skilled persons who offer services but do not have registered business. Regular users are simply all other users who neither own or represent a business nor service registered in TriniBiz, but rather consume these services or use the app as a consumer.

Biz registration requirements

Before you can register a Biz on behalf of a regular user of TriniBiz, you need to first receive an email from that user. Invite requests are received at invite@trinibiz.co (Please check everyday, if you are responsible for reviewing email invitation requests, or communicate daily with the person who is responsible for responding to the requests). A response must be sent to the user within 3 working days. The following are the details each email invite request must contain:

  • The Biz user type, whether they are a service provider, or a business owner

  • Name and address of business or service

  • Business Email, phone contact and fax # (fax # not required)

  • Website and Facebook Page (not required)

  • Services provided

Remember to email the user.

Upon registering the Biz, you should email the TriniBiz User, informing him/her that he/she can sign in TriniBiz and use it as a Biz owner.
