Different type of users

There are three types of users: Biz user (business owner or service provider) or a regular user. Business owners are users who own or represent a business registered in TriniBiz. Service Providers are skilled persons who offer services but do not have registered business. Regular users are simply all other users who neither own or represent a business nor service registered in TriniBiz, but rather consume these services or use the app as a consumer.

Biz registration requirements

Before you can register your Biz, you need to first register as a regular user of TriniBiz, through the signup menu option.

Register your Biz

Upon sigiing up, you will be able to sign in TriniBiz and use it as a regular user. If you are interested in becoming a Biz user the following information can be sent to invite@trinibiz.co, and we will respond within 3 working days:

  • Choose whether you are a service provider, or a business owner

  • Name and address of business or service

  • Business Email, phone contact and fax # (fax # not required)

  • Website and Facebook Page (not required)

  • Services provided
